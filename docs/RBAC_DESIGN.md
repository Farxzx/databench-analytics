\# Role-Based Access Control Design



\## hr\_analyst



Permissions:



\* View employee data

\* View salary information

\* View employee emails

\* No access to modify sales data



\## sales\_analyst



Permissions:



\* View sales records

\* View masked employee emails

\* View salary bands only

\* No access to raw salary information



\## executive



Permissions:



\* Read-only access

\* View summary reports

\* View department analytics

\* View sales analytics

\* No update or delete permissions
